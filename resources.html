Resources

Why are resources important?

A study of Darko Jacimovic indicates that roughly 53% of recent graduates are unemployed.An average college student needs up to 6 months to find their first employment. Even with the perfect resume, reference and other job seeking strategies,they are unlikely to find work in their field or even one that requires a college degree. Half of the people with bachelor's degree are potenitally affected by automation. This is why knowing the resources around you and utilizing these resources are important. Finding the right resources can help guide you to path beneficial for you and the community while finding a career you enjoy. Resources can help you get in contact that will push you to get to where you want to be and Year Up is the best place to capture your dreams.

Median earnings for recent graduates are lowers than they were in 1990. You can check out the study here.

Here in Year Up, there are plenty of coaches, advisors and professors that will recalculate your career compass. The only thing necessary is hard work, professionalism and perseverance.
