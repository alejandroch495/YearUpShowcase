Post Year Up

Get First Professional Job

Here in Year Up, you will have the opportunity to learn professional skills alongside your track. When you achieve high performance and you are the right fit for a company, you have a higher possibility to land a job. Year Up ask companies what skills they’re looking for, then train you in those skills to ensure you’ll land a top job when you graduate. That’s why 90% of Year Up grads are either employed within the profession they studied or in school within four months.

Continue Education

A lot of people are looking to further their education and become masters of their field. You can attend classes while going to college or you can solely focus on getting your degree. Earn college credit at one of Year Up college partners OR up to 30 ACE college credit recommendations recognized at more than 2000 higher education institutions nationwide, depending on where you train. That’s a boost to both your career and college goals.

Trying a Different Track

Here in Year Up, you have many different options. If you are in a current track and you end up not being interested or not a good match with the specific track, you are open to different options. They offer many different tracks from IT, Database, Web Security, Business, Etc.

Continued Support

In Year Up, coaches will continue to support you in every way they can. Year Up's goal does not end after you graduate. Year Up's job never ends, they always strive to help everyone become successful. There are always reasons to wait or put off your future. But Year Up can help you manage anything that challenges your success, no matter how big or small. Focus on your goals. Our coaches, mentors, social workers, and staff are here to support you. After graduating Year Up, you’ll have access to resources, support and a network of 18,000+ alumni. Our vision is to see alumni advance in their careers and education, give back to Year Up, and be the movers and shakers in the Opportunity Movement. You’re a student for a year and a Year Up graduate for life.
